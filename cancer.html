Cancer

**Cancer** is a disease in which abnormal cells grow uncontrollably in the body and can form tumors. These cells may spread to other parts of the body and affect normal body functions. Cancer can be caused by factors such as smoking, unhealthy diet, harmful chemicals, radiation, and genetic factors. Common symptoms include unusual lumps, weight loss, tiredness, and changes in the body. Early detection and healthy lifestyle habits can help in prevention and treatment.
