Heart

Heart disease is a medical condition that affects the heart and blood vessels. It can occur when the arteries become blocked or when the heart does not pump blood properly. Common causes include high blood pressure, high cholesterol, smoking, obesity, and lack of exercise. People with heart disease may experience symptoms such as chest pain, shortness of breath, and fatigue. Maintaining a healthy lifestyle with regular exercise, a balanced diet, and routine medical checkups can help prevent heart disease and keep the heart healthy. ❤️
