Digestive Issues

**Digestive Issues** are problems that affect the digestive system and make it difficult for the body to break down and absorb food properly. Common issues include indigestion, bloating, constipation, diarrhea, and stomach pain. These problems can be caused by unhealthy eating habits, stress, infections, or eating too much oily and spicy food. Symptoms may include gas, nausea, heartburn, and stomach discomfort. Eating fiber-rich foods, drinking plenty of water, and maintaining healthy eating habits can help improve digestion and prevent digestive problems.
