Obesity

Obesity is a health condition in which a person has too much body fat. It usually occurs when a person eats more food than the body needs and does not do enough physical activity. Unhealthy eating habits, fast food, sugary drinks, and lack of exercise can lead to obesity. Genetic factors and lifestyle habits may also contribute to this condition. Obesity can increase the risk of serious diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure. People who are obese may also face joint pain and breathing problems. It can affect both physical and mental health. Maintaining a healthy diet with fruits and vegetables is important. Regular exercise and an active lifestyle help control body weight. Healthy habits and regular medical checkups can help prevent obesity. ⚖️
